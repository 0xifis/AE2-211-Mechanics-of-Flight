\subsection{Basic Definitions}
There are 2 basic sources of all aerodynamic forces on any body:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item Pressure distribution over the surface of the body, $P$
  \item Shear stress distribution (ie. skin friction)
\end{enumerate}
